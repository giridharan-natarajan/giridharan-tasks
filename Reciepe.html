MALAI KOFTA

Malai Kofta is a very popular Indian vegetarian dish where balls made of potato and paneer are deep fried and served with a creamy and spiced tomato based curry. The literal translation of this dish is malai=creamy/buttery and kofta=spiced balls.

HEALTH BENIFITS

Malai Kofta is a good source of protein and fats, with the vegetarian ball rich in fibre content. The veggies used also have various nutrients, and therefore, this malai kofta recipe has many health benefits. They are Paneer malai kofta dish lowers the blood pressure levels in the body.

INGREDIANTS

  1. Onion-4
  2. Tomato-4
  3. Potato-3
  4. Panner
  5. Cream 1pkt
  6. cashew-10
  7. Oil-2tbsp
  8. coriender leaves
  9. Redchilli powder
  10. Coriander powder
  11. Garam masala
  12. Rice flour

Reciepe
  1. firstly, in a pan heat 2 tbsp oil and saute 1 onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste

  2. saute until onions changes colour slightly

  3. further add 2 tomato and saute slightly.

  4. cool completely and transfer to a blender.

  5. filter until silky smooth onion-tomato puree is attained. keep aside

  6. in a large kadai heat 1 tbsp butter and 2 tbsp oil.

  7. saute 1 tsp cumin, 2 pod cardamom, 1 bay leaf, 1 inch cinnamon, 2 clove until it turns aromatic.

  8. further keeping the flame on low, add 1 tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp turmeric, ¾ tsp coriander powder and ¼ tsp cumin powder.

  9. further add in the prepared onion tomato puree, 1 tsp salt and mix well.

  10. cover and cook until the mixture starts to thicken and oil separates from sides.

  11. finally, pour the curry over kofta and malai kofta is ready to enjoy

    12. Dish is ready enjoy!!!
